# Privacy Policy for Task Timer

**Last updated: February 2025**

## Overview

Task Timer is a standalone desktop application designed to help you track your work time locally on your device.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

- All task records are stored locally on your device only
- No data is transmitted over the network or to any external servers
- No analytics, telemetry, or usage data is collected
- No account or registration is required

## Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your computer:

- **Task records**: Stored in memory during the session
- **CSV exports**: Saved to your Documents folder (or a custom directory you specify)
- **Settings**: Stored in a local JSON file in the application directory

## Permissions Used

The application uses the following system capabilities:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **UI Automation** | To detect the current URL in your browser for automatic task tracking |
| **Process Monitoring** | To detect which applications are currently active |
| **Idle Detection** | To pause task tracking when you're away from your computer |

These capabilities are used solely for the core functionality of the application and do not transmit any data externally.

## Third-Party Services

Task Timer does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties because we do not collect any data.

## Children's Privacy

This application does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
